## Packages
date-fns | Formatting timestamps for simulation runs
react-hook-form | Form state management
@hookform/resolvers | Zod validation for forms
framer-motion | Smooth transitions for status changes

## Notes
- Theme is strictly dark mode (scientific/professional).
- Polling is enabled for simulations in 'pending' or 'running' states every 2 seconds.
- Video playback relies on `outputVideoUrl` being a valid static or dynamic URL served by the backend.
